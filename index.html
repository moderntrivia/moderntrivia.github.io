There was a new mayor and I had a flight on Spirit Airlines booked for Fort Lauderdale that afternoon. I wasn’t giving up my seat, I already paid for my hotel room. It was going to be an interesting day.

Outside I noticed what looked to be another Spirit airliner flying maybe 2000 feet down, back, and to the right of our plane. I wonder if this had something to do with our captain’s last flight for Spirit. The stewardess started to cry during her final announcement. He’d been flying commercial aircraft as long as I’d been alive. I felt like a bum. Everyone shook his hand on the way off the plane.

Man, what a clusterfuck. I feel sick again this morning. I didn’t feel that way when I woke up earlier but something broke inside me and I’d rather write this when I felt well but I don’t know when that will be.
